Welcome to Fusion Nails


We are the most beautiful and cleanest nail care salon in town! Our experienced, Friendly staff makes your visit a true experience! If you are looking to get pampered and beautify your hands and feet, then you have come to the right place.

We offer professional nail care and waxing for ladies and gentlemen. Also, we are specialists in servicing your nails for a very elegant look and a most relaxing experience. All you have to do is sit back and relax in a massaging chair with your favorite beauty magazine, soak your feet in a warm bath, and let us take care of you.

Manicure

Manicurist will turn your shabby cuticles and nails into beautiful hands. Our nail specialists will pamper you hands by first soaking them in warm water, and often times essential oils. Then the Manicurist pushes back or trims your cuticles, buffs your nail beds, files and shapes your nails. Our manicurists will also provide a gentle hand massage parlor prior to painting your nails. We also have a variety of color to chose from including the popular French Manicure. We also have option for acrylic, gel, silk, or wrap nails fro those with short nails.

Pedicure

Sit on roller massage chair while soaking your feet in a soothing whirlpool foot bath. Begins with your toe nails being cut and filed. Then the cuticles are pushed and trimmed. Your feet is then scrubbed with callus remover, and massaged with moisturizing lotion. Finally with a choice of polish for a finished presentation.

Artifical Nails

Acrylic Nails are popular because they tend to keep your nails looking newly polished, less prone to chipping compared to your natural nails, and have given women’s hands an option to have a perfect 10 at all times!

Waxing

Waxing is a form of semi-permanent hair removal which removes the hair from the root.
